Vanishing History

Meet the Team Behind the Project

Our Mission

We are dedicated to preserving archaeological sites worldwide, raising awareness about the risks of looting and vandalism, and promoting innovative solutions like Origins Guardian to safeguard history.

Our Team

Our FLL robotics team combines expertise in archaeology, robotics, and cultural heritage preservation. Our team consists of total 9 members; Himaanshu, Aishani, Advay, Aneeshwar, Mayukha, Neal, Pradyun, Samanvi, and Sourish. By leveraging technology, research, and education, we aim to protect humanity's shared past for future generations.

Our Vision

We envision a world where archaeological treasures are safeguarded and accessible for study, education, and inspiration. Through proactive monitoring, community engagement, and technological solutions, we strive to prevent destruction and theft of cultural heritage.
